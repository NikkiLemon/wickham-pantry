About Us

A membership-based scheme providing a sustainable and long term solution for the problem of food poverty in Wickham and surrounding areas.

Tuesdays 1-3 pm, 6-8 pm

This provides a helping hand for those who need it, the pantry provides £15 worth of food shopping for only £5!.

For further information please contact Wickhampantry@gmail.com.
